Dear Old Dominion Community: 

The Norfolk Police Department is investigating a shooting reported to have occurred around 11:30 p.m. yesterday in the 800 block of West 45th Street in Norfolk (near the intersection of West 45th Street and Colley Avenue). A 23-year-old man was transported to Sentara Norfolk General Hospital with non-life threatening gunshot wounds. Residents who live near this area can expect to see an increased police presence.

 

Police ask for anyone with information about this incident to call the Norfolk Crime Line at 1-888-562-5887 (1-888-LOCK-U-UP) or submit a tip through the P3 mobile app.  The Norfolk Police Department is leading this investigation. No further information is being released at this time. 

 

Jo Ann Hughes

Public Information Officer
