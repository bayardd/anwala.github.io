      Government Shutdown Support

      $2,128 of $5,000 goal

      Raised by 57 people in 1 month
      Donate Now  Share on Facebook

      Created January 4, 2019
      Fundraising Team
      on behalf of Lila Johnson
        Community   NEW YORK, NY
      See her story on CNN This woman is a government contractor who is not being paid during this shutdown.  She works as a janitor for our government offices.  Even when the shutdown ends, she won't get back pay.  She is only 1 example of this disgrace and I believe our American community can show we do support her.

      How many can say this couldn't be one of us?!

      Posted by Shera L. Haliczer
      6 days ago
          Share
       
       
      I just had a lovely call with Ms. Johnson and she finally went back to work just recently. She missed more than 2 months of pay and will never recover it. She is so appreciative of all the support. It would really be amazing to help make her whole. She's fighting hard for all her fellow contractors who all lost so much. She also wants to help others. If we can raise even more she can share with the others.

      Watch her on MSNBC: https://www.msnbc.com/ali-velshi/watch/federal-contractor-attending-sotu-we-deserve-shutdown-backpay-1437702211517

      Thanks for your support.

      I'm really hoping we hear today the crisis is over, but . . .

      So let's make sure all these amazing people who are at risk and waiting for good news know we care.

      Ms. Johnson is one of the ones who will never get back pay and this means she has not had any income for 29 days. That's impossible.

      I want to phone her today and let her know we have gotten closer to the goal.

      Will you help? Maybe instead of the $6 latte today you can donate to this lovely woman and her grandchildren? I know they so appreciate all the support.

      Thank you! And please share.

      Hi all. This horrible Shutdown isn't coming to an end. Ms. Johnson is holding strong and doing everything to take care of herself and her the 2 grandchildren she raises. She has the most amazing spirit.

      Of course, as life would have it, the other day the brakes went out on her car. It was incredible to speak with her and hear all her positive thoughts.

      Ms. Johnson is just thrilled with the generosity of everyone and really appreciates everyone.

      Please share with anyone you know. We can make this goal (and more) happen to show how we are a village helping one of our own.

      Thank you!
