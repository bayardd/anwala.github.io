Remsen, St. Mary's dominate in their postseason opener

    by Owen Siebring

    Friday, February 15th 2019
    remsen.PNG

    Remsen, IA -- The Remsen, St. Mary's basketball team absolutely owned Westwood on Thursday night, topping the Rebels 59-31 to advance to next Tuesday's District semifinals where they'll face Kingsley-Pierson.
