Boys high school roundup: Groton rallies for win over Langford Area

Groton outscored Langford Area 17-11 in the fourth quarter to ease away with a 58-52 win over Langford Area Thursday night in Langford.

The host Lions led 13-7 after the opening quarter and still clung to a 24-22 lead at the half before Groton pulled even in the third and ahead in the fourth.

Brodyn DeHoet had 15 points and 15 rebounds to lead the way for Groton. Treyton Diegel added 12 points and Cade Guthmiller had 11.

Langford Area was led by Benton Westss 16 points. Colin Frey added 10 more.
