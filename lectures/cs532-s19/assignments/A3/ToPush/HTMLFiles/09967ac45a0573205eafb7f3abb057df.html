HIGH SCHOOLS: Calvary Day beats Jenkins in baseball

 
Savannah Morning News

Thursday

Feb 14, 2019 at 11:33 PM


Jay Wetherington fanned eight batters over three innings, allowing an unearned run to notch the win for Calvary, which beat visiting Jenkins 8-4 in a non-region matchup.

Freshman Dayyan O'Neal was 2 for 3 with a triple and four RBI and Cameron Godbee was 2 for 3 with a double and an RBI for the Cavs.
