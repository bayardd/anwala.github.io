Oregon food stamp benefits to go out early for March after government shutdown
kptv.com - 2019-02-13 23:39:00
PORTLAND, OR (KPTV) - Oregon SNAP benefits will be issued early for March following the partial government shutdown, according to the Oregon Department of Human Services. Oregonians who normally receive food stamp benefits between the first and the ninth of each month will get their March allocation on March 1....
