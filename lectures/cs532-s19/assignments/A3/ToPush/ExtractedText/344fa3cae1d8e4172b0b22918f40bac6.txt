Prep spotlight: North Mac duo too much for South County
 
Thursday
Feb 14, 2019 at 10:49 PM Feb 14, 2019 at 10:49 PM
GIRARD -- Sam Mount and Tanner Wilson scored 15 points apiece to lead North Mac High School to a 67-42 win over South County on Thursday.
Grant Thoroman added 14 points for North Mac, which had 10 3-pointers, including three from Wilson and three from Mount.
North Mac led 22-8 at the end of the first quarter and 55-32 at the end of the third quarter.
Brenton Duffie led South County with 14 points.
PIASA SOUTHWESTERN 48, CARLINVILLE 24: At Piasa, Logan Rosentreter scored 12 points but it was not enough as Southwestern eased to a win.
Ryne Hanslow scored 15 points to lead Southwestern.
